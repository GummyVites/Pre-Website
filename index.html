Pre Best Friends Club

Pre is a place for you and nine of your closest friends to share the here and now. Users can use the app to check-in and share where they are, see what friends are up to, or even say a quick “hi”. By focusing on a smaller number of friends, we’re removing the noise and the social pressure of other networks so you can share what’s really happening. About Pre

Pre is a place for you and your friends to be yourselves. You share check-ins with your friends about where you are and what you’re up and really connect with the friends that matter.

  • Check-InsA way to easily share where you are and what you’re up to with friends on Pre.
  • ChatYou can chat with friends on Pre and any comments on replies are captured here as well.
  • Hi Saying “hi” is a light hearted way of seeing what friends are up to by sending them a push notification.
  • Live-MapAll your friends' check-ins on one map, tap on small circle on menubar to see!
  • Share LocationMeet up with your friends! Send them your meetup location
  • 9 FriendsYou are limited to nine Friends, Choose your best friends.
